List of what I've learnt as of Week 14

  • Learnt how Lottie is basically a JSON-based animation file format which allows designers to ship animations on any platform including web and mobile. This means that Lottie animations can be integrated into mobile apps and web pages together.
  • Learnt how to easily resize the animation such that it does not visually take up too much space on the website I'm putting the animation in. Despite resizing it, the animation itself still maintains a fairly high quality.
  • The size of a Lottie is way smaller than a PNG and a GIF, allowing for a quicker download speed and less disk space usage. Even I'm surprised, an animation smaller than even a GIF? Wow.
  • Assignment-wise, I guess I'll use a few animations with its intended purpose such as loading bar or a generic bar to measure progress of sorts.
